Coach painting

Perhaps you’re looking for a simple panel respray, dent or scratch repair or respraying, or even a full body overhaul?

What should you look for in a professional coach painting service?

  • All makes and models of coach should be catered for.
  • They should use the highest quality products, have the latest facilities and skilled technicians with years of experience.
  • The company should be EPA registered and approved by the Office of Fair Trading.

Expert coach painting company

An expert coach painting service will offer all types of paint finishes and use only high-quality products. They should match paint to manufacturers’ swatches using computerised paint mixing systems which will produce an exact colour match of all commercial vehicles. A dust-free environment will provide a finish which will match the most demanding standards.
